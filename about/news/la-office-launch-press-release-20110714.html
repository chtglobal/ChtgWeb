CHT Global Opens New Los Angeles Office

Bringing the Excellence of Global Telecommunications to Southern California and its Many Enterprises and Small Businesses



SAN JOSE, CA--(PRWEB - July 14, 2011) - CHT Global (Chunghwa Telecom Global, Inc.), the US subsidiary of Chunghwa Telecom Co., Ltd., focused on providing US and global enterprises telecommunication solutions to and from North America and the Asia-Pacific region, recently expanded their US presence by launching a new Southern California Office. The strategically placed operations center for CHT Global sales and network engineering support will serve as the hub for local market expansion and service support for the many small businesses and global enterprises situated in that region.

The Los Angeles area has long been known to be possessing vast business opportunities, especially for small businesses and global multinational enterprises. With its new Southern California presence, CHT Global seeks to promote its global IP VPN, internet bandwidth, and voice services via its strong network footprint throughout Asia’s Pacific Rim. In addition to global telecommunications services, other CHT Global services that will also be available include the new Small Business Toolkit, which enhances small business voice and data communication with website hosting, enterprise email, fax-to-email services, international calling plans, and more.

“We are excited to finally plant an office in Southern California. This will allow us to better serve our current customers as well as seek out new opportunities,” says Joe Yang, President of CHT Global. “We hope to be a strong solutions provider for small businesses as well as the gateway to Asia for the many multi-national corporations in this area, and especially to those Chinese and Taiwanese enterprises who need the bilingual support, cultural familiarity, and expertise from an Asian-American telecom.”
