Small Businesses Attain Voice Freedom with CHT Global’s Net2Communicate

Service Integrates Traditional and Digital Phone Networks for Superior Quality and Cost Savings



SAN JOSE, CA--(PRWEB - October 4, 2011) - On-the-go small businesses and entrepreneurs can now avert missing important phone calls to or from clients when out of the local office or even the country itself. CHT Global’s Net2Communicate voice service provides a truly global solution for businesses and individuals that desire a dedicated phone number with the flexibility to access a voice system that can process inbound and outbound calls from any location. Superior voice quality is also attained at a fraction of the cost by integrating today’s digital voice (VoIP) technology with traditional telephone networks. All that is needed is CHT Global’s VoIP gateway device, a traditional phone set, and connection to high speed Internet.

The Net2Communicate package includes unlimited free inbound calls and offers highly competitive long distance and global rates for outbound calls through integration with the Net2Asia calling card system. All US long distance calls are set at 2.5 cents per minute while calls to China and Taiwan (landlines) are even lower at 2 cents per minute. The entire service along with value added features such as call forwarding, call screening, and missed call email notifications costs only $30 annually with a $40 one-time charge for the CHT Global VoIP gateway device.

Key benefits of Net2Communicate include cost savings derived from reducing multiple phone lines into one singular and dedicated business number where calls can be directly accessed or automatically forwarded to other phone numbers as needed. Essentially, on-the-go small businesses and individuals have the ability to create a mobile or remote office to maintain communication with clients at any given moment whether they are situated in their local office, visiting a satellite office across the country, or traveling overseas. Outbound calls and rates also remain the same regardless of location so that the same ease, quality, and cost can be experienced in San Francisco, Los Angeles, New York, Beijing, Sydney, Paris, London, Delhi, and anywhere around world.

  • DND (Do-Not-Disturb)
  • Automatic Call Forwarding

About Net2Communicate
 Net2Communicate is part of the CHT Global’s Small Business Toolkit which is specifically designed for small businesses, entrepreneurs, and traveling businessmen. Similarly to the Net2Mail Enterprise Email and Digital Fax services that are also found in the Small Business Toolkit, Net2Communicate utilizes the Internet to provide “anywhere and anytime” office solutions. Based upon VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) technologies, Net2Communicate uniquely solves and simplifies global and multi-location voice complexities.
