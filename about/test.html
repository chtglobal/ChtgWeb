History

TimeLine

2002 Feb - Established in Santa Clara, CA as the US subsidiary of Chunghwa Telecom

2002 Mar - Officially incorporated as a CA corporation under Chunghwa Telecom Global

2002 May - Awarded Sec. 214 license by the FCC

2003 May - Received PUC license in California

2008 Jan - Moves to a new home in nearby San Jose, CA

2010 Oct - Chunghwa Telecom Global gets a new look & becomes CHT Global

2010 Dec - CHT Global launches Net2Mail Enterprise Email Hosting Solution

2011 Feb - CHT Global launches Net2Compute - a Global Cloud Computing Solution

Did You Know?

CHT Global orginally started off as Chunghwa Telecom Global, under the guidance of Chunghwa Telecom, Inc in Taiwan. CHT is the abbreviated form of Chunghwa Telecom, and is the common link between its sister companies such as CHT Singapore, CHT Japan, and other groups.
