EmPowering Your Business | Administrator Rights




For Enterprises Email Systems, the administrator acts as the gateway that directs traffic and manages everything so that the system works as planned. Net2Mail allows administrators the power to do many things from managing accounts as the master super-user account (adding and removing accounts, adding storage capacity for each account, changing passwords, etc), managing company group emails as well as outgoing mailing lists.

A key administrator ability is that Net2Mail provides the add-on service of mail backup archival. With this service, each company receives a large back-up storage for all corporate mail, creating a searchable recovery database for all corporate mail, data, and transactions.

All in all, the administrator is the portal manager, who will manage all the accounts and group lists, Add-on services for each account, as well as manage the account billings. Net2Mail's Account Administrator gives enterprises the control they need to manage their corporate mail.
