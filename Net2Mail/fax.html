Experience the Difference | Virtual Fax Service

Enterprises now have the power to send and receive digital fax through Net2Mail's Fax Mail Add-On Service. Users who subscribe to this service will receive either an US DID number (510 or 408) or a Taiwan DID number (02, 03, 04, or 07) that will allow them to receive digital faxes through that number in the form of a pdf file. In addition to receiving faxes, the fax mail system also allows the ability to take any document file on a computer and sending it out as an outgoing fax message.
