Tell Me More | What Makes Net2Mail so Good




Net2Mail though new to the American market, has been the standard in Taiwan for a number of years. Already used by over 10,000 enterprises, Net2Mail really is a top tier enterprise email service that has become available for anyone due to its affordable price and availability. In fact, the largest company using the email platform currently employs over 30,000 people!

Net2Mail is also only one of the many large scale enterprise service products provided by CHT Global. For example, Net2Asia is CHT Global's successful long distance pinless and online voice calling card. With CHT Global's years of experience as a solutions provider and the technical expertise of HiNet Group, Net2Mail is poised to make a lasting mark in the market.

As for the product itself, Net2Mail stands out from the rest due to its affordability while providing superior services. Few compare with the quality of virus protection and spam filtering, and even fewer offer the digital fax capability. Even still, Net2Mail is constantly improving and providing more and better solutions for our enterprise customers. Net2Mail is perfect for businesses of all sizes, flexible for today's mobile businesses, and price structured in an easy and simple way. So try it now, and experience the difference...
