EmPowering Your Business | Conservation = Better




Conservation is very important not only for saving business expenditures, but also for saving and protecting our environment. CHT Global is committed to the advancement of environmental issues, as well as helping our customers reduce their costs and expeditures through our technology and service solutions.

Net2Mail allows enterprises to reduce costs by foregoing the need for a physical fax machine, separate fax phone line, fax paper supplies, and replacement toner cartridges. Instead, our customers will be able to receive and store their incoming faxes on their computer hard drive, and only print it out when absolutely needed. The same goes for sending faxes, as enterprises typically need to print out their digital document in order to feed it through the fax machine. This allows for cost savings on capital, as well as operations as the process is cut in half and documents are easier to find archived away.

Most importantly though, our planet is precious, and the resources here are limited. Join CHT Global by reducing our paper consumption when possible, and limiting the waste produced from replacement ink cartridges and unnecessarily trashed paper documents. You'll find that, conservation isn't just better for your business or for the environment today, but instead, its better overall for the future and for everyone.
