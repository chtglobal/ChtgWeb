EmPowering Your Business | Mobility On the Go




Businesses are becoming global, and business operations have also become virtual. Part of running a business today requires us to be nimble and on our feet, mobile and flexible. Net2Mail offers a few solutions to make that possible. All you need is a computer and internet access in order to access your mail and even your faxes.

Net2Mail is an enterprise classed email service that allows for multi client connection. With SSL and TSL security encryption, your mail is ensured to be safe upon transmission and you will be able to receive it through our Net2Mail web interface so that any computer or mobile device can connect you to critical business information via the internet, WiFi, or network connection. This gives incredible flexibility for those on the go, with anywhere and anytime access.

The digital fax mail system adds another layer of mobility as your fax messages are no longer tied down to a physical line, nor do you need to access incoming fax from your office. Instead, with Net2Mail, businesses will be able to have fax messages populate right inside their fax mailbox, and can send any mission critical document via fax as long as they have internet connection. Once again, the power of anytime and anywhere empowers businesses with todays mobility.
