Experience the Difference | Professional Identity





With Net2Mail, enterprise email is a one stop shop. Net2Mail provides email domain name registration as a service, allowing our customers to create a professional corporate identity through their email address. Me@MyCompany.com gives a business credibility and causes the name brand to stick. In addition, Net2Mail allows for each corporate email identity to create up to 25 forwarding accounts. Which means you can purchase a single email account with Net2Mail, but also create forwarding accounts like info@MyCompany.com, Sales@MyCompany.com, CustomerService@MyCompany.com, and more... giving the impression of a large and organized company.
