Experience the Difference | Storage and Archival




Net2Mail has storage capacities available starting at 1Gb for each user account, and incrementally grows to 3Gb, 5Gb, and 10Gb. Large email storage space for webmail allows for enterprise email users to keep all email stored in the Net2Mail servers as long as there is enough space. Alternatively, enterprises can also choose the Archival System option which aggregates email transactions from all accounts into a secured storage space accessible only by the administrator account.

Storage capacity is especially important if your company sends and receives large files. For example, Net2Mail allows for the transmission of attachments up to 50Mb in size. Enterprises that choose the add-on service of Fax Mail will also need larger storage capacity to store their digital fax documents. Net2Mail gives you a surplus of storage at affordable prices so that you can archive your messages in the cloud and never worry about lost or bounced emails again.

In additin, online storage is crucial for mobility and flexible access in today's business environment. With large webmail storage accounts, businesses on the go will be able to access and retrieve archived documents from any computer or device with internet connection to the Net2Mail web portal. Find what you need anytime, anywhere, and especially when you need it the most.
