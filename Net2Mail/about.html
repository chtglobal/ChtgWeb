About Net2Mail

Net2Mail is the Enterprise-Grade Email Hosting Solution provided by CHT Global (Chunghwa Telecom Global, Inc). Originally established in Taiwan under the name hiBox by Chunghwa Telecom's HiNet Group, the robust enterprise messaging service powered the majority of businesses both small, medium, and large in the Taiwanese market. With already over 10,000 enterprise customers using the hiBox platform, Net2Mail is set to launch in America as a tried and true business email service.


About CHT Global

CHT Global is the wholly-owned US subsidiary of Chunghwa Telecom, the largest telecommunication company in Taiwan. Since 2002, CHT Global has helped numerous enterprises achieve their targeted goals while reducing customer side complexity by providing industry leading multi-site network solutions through its global backbone infrastructure and world-class service. CHT Global is currently headquartered in San Jose, CA, serving many of the major companies residing in the Silicon Valley and around the world.
