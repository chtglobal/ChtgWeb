Trans-Pacific Submarine Cable Lines

Gain Access to the Widest International Cable Line Network in he Pacific and Beyond

CHT Global's Data Wholesale includes the leasing of its undersea submarine cables within its network across the Pacific Ocean. Also known as IPLC Project (10G/2.5G) which stands for International Private Line Cables with capacities from 2.5G up to 10G, carriers will be able to access one of the widest variety of cross-Pacific channels available, while also having the opportunity to reach multiple locations worldwide throughout CHT Global's network.
