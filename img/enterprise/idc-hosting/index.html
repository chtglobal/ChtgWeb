IDC Hosting

Why IDC?

CHT Global's IDC service is ideal for enterprises and service providers who intend to consolidate their facilities and appplications in a secured facility. In otherwords, enterprises will be able to outsource the storage, bandwidth provision, and facilities management to CHT Global. Customers will find their data and applications stored within facilities with stable environments, constant power, and high level security.

With coverage spanning North America (USA), Asia (Taiwan, Japan, Hong Kong, Vietnam, and Singapore), Australia, and Europe (Netherlands, and the UK), CHT Global's footprint continues to expand worldwide. Within carrier-neutral Internet Data Centers, CHT Global allows customers easy and convenient access to unlimited bandwidth. In addition, multi-site deployment with customizable bandwidth maximizes redundancy and is the best solution for multinational enterprise, allowing them to expand their reach across the globe with guaranteed success.

Key Features

  • Global Coverage: CHT Global's IDC network of 24 data site locations spanning 12 countries worldwide
  • Controlled Environments: Uninterrupted Power, Cooling, Fire Suppression, & Physical Security
  • High Speed Connection: Access to CHT Global Network with direct connection to Asia
  • Suite of Managed and Value Added Services
  • 24/7/365 Hands-OnSupport

Benefits

  • Room to Grow: Space and resources expansion available to grow as you grow
  • Unparalleled Customer Support: 24/7 and 365 days of customer focused support
