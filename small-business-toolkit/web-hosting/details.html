Net2Mail Email Hosting

Enterprise Quality Security

CHT Global’s Net2Mail Email Hosting Solution comes with industry leading IronPort Anti-Spam and Symantec Anti-Virus Protection. Today’s messaging communication is filled with dangerous and potentially devastating viruses that can cripple business operations temporarily if not for longer periods. In addition, spam can be a great hindrance to productivity, and may even lead to the possibility of losing important email lost among mounds of spam junk mail . Thus, when profitability depends so much on time sensitive communication and constant communication, email failure is something businesses simply cannot afford.

With Net2Mail, users can have confidence that they will not only receive all important incoming enterprise mail, but also block out unwanted spam mail as well. Seeing a clean email box never looked better and diving straight into work never seemed easier. Businesses can now focus their energy on the important things and what they do best: business engagement and product development -- while letting CHT Global act as the supporting engine to achieve those goals.

Enterprise Quality Collaboration

Collaboration is a key component of every present day enterprise. Net2Mail Email Hosting Solution offers the tools needed for enterprises to function efficiently and effectively both internally and externally with customers and partners. Included in this service is Calendar Sharing, Folder Sharing, and even Fax Capabilities for sending out digital fax messages as well as receiving inbound faxes to be stored right into your Net2Mail email box.

Enterprise Quality You Can Count On

CHT Global’s global network and datacenters guarantee transmission redundancy and safe storage. But on top of that, customers can add on Net2Mail Cloud-Based Email Archiving, allowing for real-time backup as well as anytime retrieval of critical mail messages. In addition, customers will also receive dedicated service and support from CHT Global’s worldwide support team; meaning the availability of top of the line 24/7 service in both English and Mandarin.


Solution Spotlight

Basic WebMail offers a convenient solution to email message management. Many provide some form of virus protection and spam filtering, but most of the time it is a basic form of service. Like they always say: "You get what you pay for." CHT Global's Net2Mail solution is protected with advanced Symantec and Ironport security technology, meaning you're getting the top of the line filtering and scanning options available to keep your corporate messaging experience as clean and fluid as possible.
